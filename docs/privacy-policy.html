✨ Highlighter Plus Privacy Policy ✨

This privacy policy describes how Highlighter Plus ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Limitations

Please Note:

  • Storage: Chrome's storage API has limits - large amounts of highlights may require cleanup
  • Compatibility: May not work on some dynamic websites or protected content
  • Performance: Large documents may experience slight delays in highlighting
  • PDF Export: Complex page layouts may not export perfectly
  • Data Persistence: Clearing browser data will remove all highlights

Information Collection and Use

Highlighter Plus collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Text highlights you create while using the extension
  • The URLs of pages where you create highlights
  • Timestamps of when highlights were created
  • Color preferences for your highlights

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit, collect, or store any of your data on external servers. Your highlights remain private and under your control.

Data Access

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your highlights locally
  • Tabs: To access the current page URL for organizing highlights
  • Downloads: To enable PDF export functionality

Third-Party Services

The extension uses the following third-party libraries:

  • jsPDF: For PDF generation (client-side only)
  • Google Fonts: For text rendering in the extension interface

Data Retention and Deletion

Your highlights and settings are retained until you:

  • Manually delete individual highlights
  • Clear your browser data
  • Uninstall the extension

Children's Privacy

Highlighter Plus does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension or the Chrome Web Store listing.
